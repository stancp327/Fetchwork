# FetchWork Community Guidelines

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

FetchWork is built on trust. These guidelines help keep our community safe, fair, and productive for everyone.

---

## Be Honest

- **Accurate profiles:** Your skills, experience, portfolio, and reviews must be truthful
- **Realistic expectations:** Set fair prices, honest timelines, and deliverable scopes
- **No fake reviews:** Don't buy, trade, or fabricate reviews — every review must be from a real completed transaction
- **No misleading listings:** Job posts and service listings must accurately describe the work

---

## Be Professional

- **Communicate clearly:** Respond to messages in a reasonable timeframe. If you can't take a job, say so
- **Deliver what you promise:** Complete work according to the agreed scope, timeline, and quality
- **Respect deadlines:** If you'll be late, communicate early — don't ghost
- **Handle disagreements maturely:** Use our dispute resolution process, not threats or harassment

---

## Be Respectful

- **No harassment:** Threats, bullying, intimidation, or unwanted contact of any kind
- **No discrimination:** Based on race, gender, religion, age, disability, sexual orientation, national origin, or any protected characteristic
- **No hate speech:** Slurs, derogatory language, or content promoting hatred
- **Respect boundaries:** A "no" is a "no" — in hiring, in messaging, in everything

---

## Keep Payments on Platform

This is critical for your protection:

- ✅ **DO** use FetchWork's payment system for all transactions
- ❌ **DON'T** request or send payments via Venmo, Zelle, Cash App, PayPal, wire transfer, cryptocurrency, or gift cards
- ❌ **DON'T** ask to "work off-platform" to avoid fees

**Why?** Our payment protection only works when payments go through FetchWork. Off-platform payments have zero protection — no refunds, no dispute resolution, no proof of payment.

---

## Protect Your Safety

### Don't Share Too Early
- Don't share your phone number, email, or home address in initial messages
- Use FetchWork messaging until you've established trust
- For local jobs, meet in public or well-trafficked areas first

### Red Flags to Watch For
- 🚩 Asked to pay upfront before any work agreement
- 🚩 "Send a deposit via [non-FetchWork method]"
- 🚩 Job pays unusually high for minimal work
- 🚩 Pressure to move communication off-platform immediately
- 🚩 Requests for personal financial information (SSN, bank login, etc.)
- 🚩 "Mystery shopper" or "payment processing" jobs
- 🚩 Asking you to buy gift cards or forward packages

**If something feels off, trust your gut and report it.**

---

## Prohibited Content & Activities

### Never Allowed
- Illegal services or goods
- Adult content or services
- Weapons, drugs, or controlled substances
- Counterfeit goods or services
- Multi-level marketing or pyramid schemes
- Spam, phishing, or malware distribution
- Services that violate others' intellectual property
- Account selling or sharing

### Content Standards
- No copyrighted material without permission
- No personally identifiable information of others
- No violent, graphic, or disturbing content
- Portfolio work must be your own (or clearly credited)

---

## For Clients

- **Pay fairly:** Offer reasonable compensation for the work requested
- **Provide clear requirements:** Vague job posts lead to disputes — be specific about what you need
- **Respond to deliveries:** Review and approve (or request revisions) in a timely manner
- **Don't ask for free work:** Spec work, "tests," or unpaid trials are not allowed as a condition of hiring
- **Respect the freelancer's expertise:** You hired them for a reason

---

## For Freelancers

- **Only accept work you can deliver:** Don't overcommit
- **Communicate proactively:** Update clients on progress, especially if issues arise
- **Deliver original work:** Plagiarism or recycled deliverables are grounds for account removal
- **Respect client confidentiality:** Don't share client information or project details without permission
- **Handle revisions professionally:** Reasonable revisions within scope are expected

---

## Reporting & Enforcement

### How to Report
- Use the **Report** button on any profile, message, job, or service listing
- Email trust@fetchwork.net with details
- Reports are confidential — we don't reveal who reported

### What Happens
1. Our team reviews every report
2. We may contact involved parties for more information
3. Actions range from warnings to permanent account removal, depending on severity

### Consequences
- **First violation (minor):** Warning + education
- **Second violation or serious first offense:** Temporary suspension (7-30 days)
- **Severe violation:** Permanent ban + forfeiture of pending platform credits
- **Illegal activity:** Permanent ban + referral to law enforcement
