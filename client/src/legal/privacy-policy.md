# FetchWork Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: [DATE]**

FetchWork ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account information:** Name, email address, password, profile picture
- **Profile information:** Skills, bio, portfolio items, location, hourly rate
- **Payment information:** Processed by Stripe — we do not store your full credit card number
- **Tax information:** W-9 or W-8BEN forms for payout compliance
- **Communications:** Messages sent through the platform, support requests
- **Verification documents:** Government-issued ID for identity verification (stored securely, deleted after verification)

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Usage data:** Pages visited, features used, search queries, time spent
- **Device information:** Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
- **IP address:** Used for security, fraud prevention, and approximate location
- **Cookies:** See Section 6

### Information from Third Parties
- **Google/Facebook login:** Name, email, profile picture (only what you authorize)
- **Stripe:** Payment confirmation status (not your full card details)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide the platform** — Create accounts, match freelancers with clients, process payments
- **Communicate with you** — Service updates, job notifications, booking confirmations, support responses
- **Improve the platform** — Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, develop new features
- **Ensure safety** — Detect fraud, enforce our Terms of Service, verify identities
- **Legal compliance** — Tax reporting (1099-NEC), respond to legal requests
- **Marketing** — With your consent, send promotional emails (you can opt out anytime)

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your messages with anyone outside the conversation
- Use your data for purposes unrelated to FetchWork

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We share your information only in these circumstances:

### With Other Users
- **Public profile information:** Name, profile picture, bio, skills, ratings, reviews, portfolio
- **Job/service details:** Visible to potential matches
- **Messages:** Shared only with conversation participants

### With Service Providers
- **Stripe:** Payment processing
- **Cloudinary:** Image hosting
- **MongoDB Atlas:** Database hosting
- **Render:** Application hosting
- **Sentry:** Error tracking (no personal data, only technical errors)
- **Google Analytics:** Usage analytics (anonymized)

### For Legal Reasons
- To comply with law, regulation, or legal process
- To protect the rights, safety, or property of FetchWork, our users, or the public
- In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to you)

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption:** All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS
- **Password hashing:** Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we cannot see your password
- **Access controls:** Employee access to user data is limited and logged
- **Payment security:** Card data is handled entirely by Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant) — we never see or store your full card number
- **Verification documents:** Stored in secure, access-controlled cloud storage; deleted after verification is complete

No system is 100% secure. If we discover a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you promptly as required by law.

## 5. Your Rights & Choices

### All Users
- **Access your data:** View and download your information from your account settings
- **Update your data:** Edit your profile, email, and preferences anytime
- **Delete your account:** Request account deletion from settings or by emailing support@fetchwork.net
- **Opt out of marketing:** Unsubscribe link in every marketing email
- **Control notifications:** Manage notification preferences in account settings

### California Residents (CCPA)
You have the right to:
- Know what personal information we collect and why
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Opt out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell it)
- Not be discriminated against for exercising your rights

To exercise these rights, email privacy@fetchwork.net or use the in-app data request feature.

### EU/UK Residents (GDPR)
If applicable, you have additional rights including:
- Right to access, rectify, and erase your data
- Right to restrict or object to processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to withdraw consent

Contact our data protection contact at privacy@fetchwork.net.

## 6. Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| **Essential** | Login sessions, security, preferences | Session / 30 days |
| **Analytics** | Usage patterns, page views (anonymized) | Up to 2 years |
| **Functional** | Remember your settings, recent searches | Up to 1 year |

We do **not** use advertising or tracking cookies.

### Managing Cookies
You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

## 7. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:
- **Active accounts:** As long as your account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** Personal data deleted within 30 days; transaction records retained for 7 years (tax/legal compliance)
- **Messages:** Retained for the duration of your account; deleted with account deletion
- **Tax documents (1099, W-9):** Retained for 7 years as required by IRS regulations
- **Verification documents:** Deleted within 90 days of verification completion
- **Server logs:** Retained for 90 days
